Introduction

ChemTree is an Open Source interactive application for visualizing chemical similarity/diversity in a molecule library. The visualization part of ChemTree is built almost entirely of AngularJS and D3.

Features

  • Circle border, size, and color can all be mapped to chosen properties in the data.
  • Dragging and zooming.
  • Searching by ligand ID.
  • Server side platform neutral implementation. Can be ported to PHP, JSP easily.

What can you do with ChemTree

  • Comparing multiple molecular libraries
  • QSAR studies
  • Organize molecule related data

Some tips about using ChemTree

  • We recommend Chrome and Firefox as your browser
  • You can click on the leaf to activate the force directed graph
  • After activating force directed graph, the location for each node will be adjusted. So, please do not suprise about nodes going around
  • For more than 3000 nodes, the force directed graph will be slow...
  • You can always drag the whole tree by dragging the white space, and zoom with middle mouse button.
